kubectl add — examples

Manifests served for kubectl add. Apply any of them server-side:

kubectl add https://scaffoldly.github.io/kubectl-add/nginx.yaml

Available

  • nginx.yaml — nginx Deployment + Service
  • configmap.yaml — a demo ConfigMap
  • kustomization.yaml — a kustomization pulling nginx + hello over Pages

Remove any of them with --remove:

kubectl add --remove https://scaffoldly.github.io/kubectl-add/nginx.yaml
